🗣️ Exercise 5: Speaking Practice

  1. What will you do if it rains this weekend?
  2. If you could travel anywhere in the world, where would you go and why?
  3. What would you do if you won one million dollars?
  4. If you had studied a different major, what job would you have now?
  5. What would you have done differently if you had failed an important exam?
  6. What happens if people don't get enough sleep?
  7. What would happen if everyone stopped using smartphones?
  8. If you could change one thing about your hometown, what would it be?
  9. If you become fluent in English, how will your life change?
  10. If you had the chance to meet any famous person, who would it be and why?

Follow-up Questions

  • Can you explain your answer?
  • Can you give an example?
  • What are the advantages and disadvantages?
  • Do you think everyone would agree with you?
  • How would your family or friends react?
